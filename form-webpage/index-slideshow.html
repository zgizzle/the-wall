• Welcome to the Smart Wall

    The Smart Wall lets you post a note on a virtual wall that can be viewed using Augmented Reality. When you post to The Smart Wall we will use machine learning to analyse the sentiment of everything posted on the wall. This mood will be shown on the website dashboard. Treat the Smart Wall wall as a public place. Your posts will be visible to others with access to the Smart Wall using augmented reality. Your post will be reviewed before it goes live check the moderation policy (link to moderation policy) before you post.

  • About The Smart Wall

    The Smart Wall is a project of postgraduate students in the 3A Institute Masters Courses in the College of Engineering and Computer Science at Australian National University. We are conducting research as part of our coursework (CECS8001), where our aim is to better understand how to create, maintain, and govern cyber-physical systems (a type of technological system). We do this by researching systems that already exist, and by building new systems. The Primary Investigator leading this project is Distinguished Professor Genevieve Bell, Director of the 3A Institute.

  • Data Usage

    You do not need to register to use The Smart Wall. The only data that will be stored is the content of the message, the colour you select for the note, the date the note was posted and how long you want the message to stay on the wall.

  • Contact Us

    We would appreciate any suggestions or feedback you have about using the Smart Wall, the website and the augmented reality.
